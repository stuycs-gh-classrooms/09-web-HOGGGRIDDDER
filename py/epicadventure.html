Destination: Windy-Desert

Mission Goal: Explore the "Death Zone" in the Windy-Desert

Everyone in the spaceship has been talking about the "Death Zone" that is located in the middle of the Windy-desert

Rumors has it that the remains of a mighty dragon has been seen there. Getting one will surely fetch a high price

I believe the name "Death Zone" was given to this spot because of the strong and ferocious winds that pass through there. Other than that, I believe you wont have any trouble traveling there.

Go and explore the "Death Zone" and report back to me if you find anything there

Good luck Solider

Caution

Just as the name suggests, this is not your typical desert.

Winds here are significantly stronger than winds in your average desert.

During the nighttime, winds will be extremely strong and dangerous. Getting caught up in one will not end well.
