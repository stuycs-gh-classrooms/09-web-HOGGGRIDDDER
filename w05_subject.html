    • The Twins

    "This is going to be a terrible night..." signals the natural spawning of the Twins

    Can be summoned using the mechanical eye at night

    Exclusive Boss Drops

    • Retinazer Trophy (10%)
    • Spazmatism Trophy (10%)
    • Souls of Sight (25-40) (100%)
    • Twin Mask (14%)

      • Forms of the Twins

      Retinazer (Top) and Spazmatism (Bottom). When each eye's hp reaches low than 50%, they transform into their respective forms (Right). Retinazer lazers shoot faster with higher damage. Spazmatism gains a extremely fast dash and spits a continous line of fire for a short time.

      • Souls of Might (25-40) (100%)
      • Destroyer Mask (14%)

        • The destroyer is a mechanical worm-type boss similar to the Eater of Worlds. The Destroyer is made of of multiple segments and each of its segments shoots out lasers and launches out Probes that also shoots out lasers
