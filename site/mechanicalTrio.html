TERRARIA HARDCORE MECHANICAL TRIO

The Twins

Stats

  • Health: 82237
  • Defense: 10
  • Knock-back resist: 100%

Immunity

  • Poisoned
  • Confused

Forms of The Twins

First Form

The Twins is a much more difficult version of the Eye of Cthulhu.

Instead of one eyeball, there are two eyeballs this time.

One with the red iris is called Retinazer. Other with green iris is called Spazmatism.

Retinazer attacks consist of shooting quick purple lasers and usually floats diagonally above the player

Spazmatism is much more aggressive. Its attacks consist of medium-speed green fire balls and usually floats near the player

Both of the eyeballs are able to dash towards the player, inflicting melee damage

Retinazer (First Form) Stats

  • Health: 38250
  • Damage: 114 (Both melee and Eye Laser)
  • Defense: 10
  • Knock-back resist: 100%

Spazmatism (First Form) Stats

  • Health: 43987
  • Damage: 127 (melee), 132 (Cursed Flame)
  • Defense: 10
  • Knock-back resist: 100%

Debuffs

  • Cursed Inferno (68.75%) (Duration: 5-7.5 secs)
    • -12 health points per second
    • No health regeneration
    • Takes 10% more knockback

Second Form

When the health of each Twin reaches below 40%, the Twin changes into its second form.

In Retinazer's second form, it becomes a mechanical eyeball with a laser cannon coming out of its pupil.

Retinazer's attacks now shoots out Death Lasers that are much faster and deal much more damage

Retinazer also periodically shoots out a huge barrage of low-damaging lasers towards the player now

In Spazmatism's second form, it becomes a mechanical eyeball with its pupil enveloped inside, making a mouth full of fangs coming out.

Spazmatism's speed increases and its dashes are significantly faster with greater range.

Spazmatism's now bursts out streams of cursed flame, like a flamethrower, towards the player. It inflicts cursed inferno debuff on touch

Retinazer (Second Form) Stats

  • Health: 15300
  • Damage: 171 (melee), 138 (Death Laser), 102 (rapid fire)
  • Defense: 20
  • Knock-back resist: 100%

Spazmatism (Second Form) Stats

  • Health: 17594
  • Damage: 190 (melee), 162 (Eye Fire)
  • Defense: 28
  • Knock-back resist: 100%

Debuffs

  • Cursed Inferno (68.75%) (Duration: 5-7.5 secs)
    • -12 health points per second
    • No health regeneration
    • Takes 10% more knockback

Summoning

The Twins can randomly spawn with a 10% chance at dusk under these conditions:

  • Demon/Crimson Altar has been destroyed
  • It has not been defeated yet in the world

When The Twins are spawned, the player is greeted with the message "This is going to be a terrible night..."

Mechanical Eye

The Twins can be manually summoned using the Mechanical Eye at night.

Mechanical Eye can be crafted using the following items on a Mythril/Orichalcum Anvil:

  • Lens x3
  • Iron/Lead Bar x5
  • Soul of Light x6

Honorable Exclusive Boss Drops

  • Treasure bag (Item) (100%)
    • Mechanical Wheel Piece (Material) (100%)
    • Soul of Sight (Material) (100%)
    • Hallowed Bar (Material) (100%)
  • Pair of Eyeballs (Pet) (25%)
  • Twins Relic (Vanity) (100%)

Additional Information of the Boss

The Twins can be only fought during nighttime. If the day becomes daytime, The Twins will despawn.
