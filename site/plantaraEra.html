TERRARIA Mechanical Trio

The Twins

Stats

  • HP: 64500
  • DEFENSE: 10

"This is going to be a terrible night..." signals the natural spawning of the Twins

Can be summoned using the mechanical eye at night

Exclusive Boss Drops

  • Retinazer Trophy (10%)
  • Spazmatism Trophy (10%)
  • Souls of Sight (25-40) (100%)
  • Twin Mask (14%)

Forms of the Twins

Retinazer (Top) and Spazmatism (Bottom). When each eye's hp reaches low than 50%, they transform into their respective forms (Right). Retinazer lazers shoot faster with higher damage. Spazmatism gains a extremely fast dash and spits a continous line of fire for a short time.

The Destroyer

Stats

  • HP: 120000
  • DEFENSE: N/A

"You feel vibrations from deep below..." signals the natural spawning of The Destroyer

Can be summoned using the mechanical worm at night

Exclusive Boss Drops

  • Destroyer Trophy (10%)
  • Souls of Might (25-40) (100%)
  • Destroyer Mask (14%)

The destroyer is a mechanical worm-type boss similar to the Eater of Worlds. The Destroyer is made of of multiple segments and each of its segments shoots out lasers and launches out Probes that also shoots out lasers

Skeletron Prime

Stats

  • HP: 42000
  • DEFENSE: 24 , 48 (while spinning)

"The air is getting colder around you..." signals the natural spawning of Skeletron Prime

Can be summoned using the mechanical skull at night

Exclusive Boss Drops

  • Skeletron Prime Trophy (10%)
  • Souls of Fright (25-40) (100%)
  • Skeletron Prime Mask (14%)

Skeletron Prime is an upgraded version of Skeletron with 4 arms and a cannon, saw, vice, and laser attached to each one respectively. Skeletron Primes attacks by swinging his arms and periodically spinning his spiked head towards the player.
