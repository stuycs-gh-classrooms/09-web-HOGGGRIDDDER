TERRARIA BOSSES

This webpage will cover the basic information such as stats, drops and summoning for each of the following bosses:

King Slime

King Slime takes the appearance of a much bigger version of a blue slime.

It wears a gold crown on its head and has a ninja npc trapped inside its body.

Eye of Cthulhu

Eye of Cthulhu is a gigantic floating demon eye ball that is able to summon smaller demon eyes and dash towards the player.

It is one of the first bosses that the player encounters and it has 2 forms.

Eater of Worlds

The Eater of Worlds is a worm-like creature made up of multiple heads that each have their own health points.

The Eater of Worlds can only be found in Corruption generated worlds.

Brain of Cthulhu

The Brain of Cthulhu is a giant floating brain with 2 forms.

The Brain of Cthulhu can only be found in Crimson generated worlds.

The Twins

The Twins is a much more difficult version of Eye of Cthulhu.

It has two eyeballs, each doing different attacks.

The Twins is part of the mechanical hardcore bosses.

The Destroyer

The Destroyer is the mechanical version of the Eater of Worlds.

It is a gigantic mechanical worm that summons probes to shoot lasers towards the player.

The Destroyer is part of the mechanical hardcore bosses.

Skeletron Prime

Skeletron Prime is the mechanical and much difficult version of Skeletron.

Its main body is a metal skeleton head and it has 4 limbs, each with different attacks.

Skeletron Prime is part of the mechanical hardcore bosses.

Mechdusa

An abomination of all the mechanical bosses combined.

It is a secret boss, found only in the "Everything" seed.

It has the most health in the game, more than the final boss, Moon Lord.
