Project description

This an adventure-type project that takes place in a desert from outter space.

Players will start on an Oasis (base startpoint of the adventure) and they will go on adventure, traversaling through the desert in search of treasure.

The project works by giving the player options to choose and advancing the story line forward base on the options chose.

The adventure will end when the player successfully completes their mission or if the player dies.

Weather will be a major factor in this adventure.

As the players traverse, they will need to keep notice of the weather status, whether it is calm or getting harsher

The player only has 1 life so the options they choose will need to be carefully thoughtof or else they will meet their end.

When a player dies, they will have to restart the adventure all over again
